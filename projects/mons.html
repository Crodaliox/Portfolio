MONS INTERNSHIP

For my final year internship as part of my engineering studies, I am currently working at the University of Mons in Belgium in the field of Artificial Intelligence. My internship, which runs from March 10 to August 29, 2025, focuses on designing and developing demonstrators that integrate AI technologies into audiovisual workflows, using embedded platforms and real-time systems.

Throughout the internship, I am tasked with comparing and selecting AI models for audio and video processing (such as noise, speech, and object detection). I also work on optimizing these models to enable real-time performance on low-power devices, using techniques like model compression with PyTorch and TensorRT.

A key part of the project involves integrating AI solutions into audiovisual production environments. This includes implementing robot control systems (such as pan-tilt robots) and developing plugin interfaces for production software. I also work on stereo vision and stereoscopic image processing to enhance 3D perception. Using stereoscopic setups, I implement camera tracking by averaging detected angles from defined points on each image, leveraging YOLO Pose for precise detection. Additionally, I explore tracking based on motion vectors
